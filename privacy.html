Speed Car Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

What Speed Car Collects

Speed Car lets players create an account with an email address and password. The game stores the player's display name and best score so the shared leaderboard can work across phones, tablets, and computers.

How Data Is Used

Account and leaderboard data is used only to sign players in, save scores, show the leaderboard, and keep the game working across devices.

Services Used

Speed Car uses Supabase for account login and cloud leaderboard storage. The Android version includes Google AdMob support. During development it uses test ads. When real ads are enabled, Google may process advertising data according to Google's advertising and privacy policies.

Children and Families

The game is designed to be family friendly. Advertising requests are configured with child-directed and under-age-of-consent treatment by default until the final Google Play target audience decision is made.

Deleting Data

To request account or leaderboard deletion, contact the developer with the email address used for the account. The matching account profile and score can then be removed from the game database.
